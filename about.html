About

Data analyst focused on reporting, operations, and practical automation.

I’m Sayr Olivares. I work at the intersection of analysis, operations, and automation, using data to make day-to-day decisions clearer and systems easier to run.

My work combines Python, data analysis, mathematics, business context, and process thinking to turn messy information into something useful for teams. I’m also building Diverum, an automation business focused on helping clinics in Bogota reduce manual work and run better systems.
